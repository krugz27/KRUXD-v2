Critique quality is mostly a facilitation problem.

When sessions anchor on goals, evidence, and constraints, feedback becomes actionable instead of subjective. I use a lightweight format that separates observation, interpretation, and recommendation.

Teams leave with clearer decisions and faster iteration cycles.
