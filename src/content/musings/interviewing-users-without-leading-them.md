publishDate: 2026-01-28

summary: Simple moderation shifts can dramatically improve research signal quality.
featured: true
---
Leading prompts create false confidence and weak product decisions.

I prefer neutral language, concrete follow-ups, and task reconstruction over opinion polling. This reveals real behavior and tradeoffs instead of aspirational answers.

A small interview guide rewrite often improves signal more than recruiting more participants.
