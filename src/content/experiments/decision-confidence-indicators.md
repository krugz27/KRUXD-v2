This concept focused on trust calibration in recommendation-heavy interfaces.

I tested lightweight confidence indicators, explanatory hover states, and reversible actions to reduce the fear of committing too early.

The resulting pattern set provided a clearer language for uncertainty while preserving a clean interaction flow.
