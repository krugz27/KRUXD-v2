The intake journey spanned digital forms, staff handoffs, and in-person checkpoints.

I mapped service touchpoints to find friction between systems and teams, then proposed an intake model with clearer status visibility and fewer redundant steps.

The result was improved form completion rates and less manual rework for front-desk staff.
