Learners struggled to find relevant courses beyond homepage recommendations.

I reworked taxonomy and filtering patterns, introduced stronger metadata cues, and improved browse-to-enroll continuity across devices.

Post-launch data showed better discovery depth and higher click-through into course detail pages.
